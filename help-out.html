How Can You Help?

Invite, Purchase, Share

  1. Invite your favorite restaurants to participate.
  2. Purchase meals for healthcare workers.
  3. Share the opportunity with your community to make more impact.

Invite

Invite Your Favorite Restaurants To Participate

Let your favorite restaurants know you care about their future. Reach out to them via e-mail, phone numbers, or social media handles (#shelter-in-plates #sip).

Need help reaching out to them? Use one of these templates or send an email to eric@shelter-in-plates.com and we will do it for you.

Purchase

Purchase Meals For Healthcare Workers

You can select to purchase meals from any participating restaurant here! Just select how many you would like to purchase, add a personal note to the restaurant and health care workers, and we will take care of the rest.

We will let you know when your order is received and when it is delivered to a local hospital in the next few days.

Share

Share the opportunity with your community to make more impact

At a time like this, we find power in uniting efforts across our communities. The more meals purchased, the more restaurants receive business, and the more health care workers stay nourished by you.

Encourage your friends, family, neighbors, and co-workers to join in our movement.

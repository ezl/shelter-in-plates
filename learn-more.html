How Does It Work?

We want to make this as easy as possible for restaurants.

  • We will set up everything for you. We'll market to your customers on your behalf, collect payment, tell you how many meals you should prepare.
  • You batch orders, so they aren't "on demand" and we will deliver them for you.
  • This will cost you nothing - We charge no fees.

Read on for more details.

Sign Up

Sign-Up via our simple intake form or reach out directly to eric@shelter-in-plates.com if that’s easier.

We know time is of the essence for you, and we don’t want to put you through extra processing. We’ll need the following to help you get started:

  • Any custom content you would like posted on your personalized page. For example, a letter to your diners, information about the meal you’ll prepare, a photo, etc.
  • Your price for an individual meal.
  • Your preferred method of contact to handle orders with us.
  • Your preferred method to receive digital payment from us.

Order Handling & Payment

  • We will compile orders for your restaurant at the end of each day and send them to you the following day.
  • Once you agree to fulfill the order, we will transfer the diner’s payment directly to you.
  • You will have 3 days to complete the order.
  • We will help you track the running total of orders pending and completed for you as we move forward together.

Delivery

  • When we send the order we will include the local hospital that is expecting the delivery.
  • If you are able to deliver the order yourself, that’s great!
  • If you need help with delivery, we will arrange a courier to pick up the order at an agreed upon time.
